Introduction

Chonky Quickfire ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Chonky Quickfire browser extension (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personal data from users of the Chonky Quickfire extension. All data related to user preferences and favorites is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Local Storage

The Chonky Quickfire extension uses the browser's local storage to save user preferences and favorites. This data is stored on your device and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Chonky Quickfire extension fetches GIFs from Giphy. We do not collect or store any data from your interactions with Giphy.

Security

The security of your data is important to us. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
